Medicine Buddha

Healing Buddha holding a jar of medicinal nectar, 12" tall with blue enamel details.

The Medicine Buddha statue is depicted in the classic full lotus posture. In his right hand, he holds the stem of the myrobalan plant between his thumb and index finger, while his left hand cradles a bowl of Lapis nectar. His right hand also displays the Varada Mudra, or 'boon-granting' gesture, symbolizing the Buddha's compassionate offering of healing to all sentient beings.

Why You’ll Love It

Healer of Body and Mind – Represents the power of spiritual and physical healing
